Ackee & Saltfish is a traditional breakfast Jamaican dish that is very popular in Caribbean culture. Ackee is a fruit that grows on Evergreen trees year round in Jamaica. Ackee is also revered as the national fruit in Jamaica. Saltfish is a cod-like fish that is very salty and completely dry. The saltfish has to be boiled prior to cooking the ackee to remove most of the salt and to give the fish some moisture.
