Little Jamaica Restaurant and Lounge

Ranked #1 Caribbean Restaurant in Killeen Texas

Little Jamaica Restaurant and Lounge is a black-owned family restaurant that opened up to the general public on February 1st, 2020. Despite opening right before Covid hit America, Little Jamaica has seen massive success with its authentic Caribbean food prepared by Jamaican chefs. Little Jamaica strongly -believes that the customer is always right.

"Okay so I've been wanting some authentic Caribbean food. Jamaican food. So I set out on a journey to find it. It was a 40/45 minute drive here from my home, but let's just say, I'll be back. The food was delicious. I'm glad I came and was able to take some home for later. Yummy!"
- Lakisha T
