Little Jamaica Restaurant and Lounge

Reviews

Alexandre Boland

First visit and you know me super honest is the best policy. My biggest complaint would be it took twenty minutes to get food that is already pre-prepped! The food was Luke warm and it should have been hot at least with that long of a waits. We were given a cup of ice with one sip of spite in it. The cashier was knowledge and very kind during our order. She explained to me what I was ordering and delivered our food with a smile. I am interested in the night life but I hope they fix the ceiling because it looks like it’s ready to cave in one spot. The dining room was clean and I loved the music. Curry chicken is in point in ever sense of the word jerk chicken was a tidbit spicy for my Palate but manageable. Plantains are very sweet I don’t do a lot of sweets anyways but they were tasty and I enjoyed my entire meal. CoCo Bread tasted like a regular dinner roll to me nothing amazing honestly but overall I will recommend to others very yummy
